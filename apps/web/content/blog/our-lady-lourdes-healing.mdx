In 1858, a 14-year-old girl named **Bernadette** saw a "Beautiful Lady" in a grotto (Massabielle).
The Lady asked her to dig in the mud. A spring bubbled up.
Since then, 70 **miracles** (medically inexplicable cures) have been officially recognized, and thousands more reported.

## The Name
The priest didn't believe Bernadette. He told her: "Ask the Lady her name."
On March 25 (Feast of the Annunciation), the Lady said:
**"I am the Immaculate Conception."**
Bernadette didn't know this dogma (declared just 4 years earlier by the Pope). It was the proof the priest needed.
