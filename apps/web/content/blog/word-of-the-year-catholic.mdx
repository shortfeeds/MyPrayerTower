Resolutions are about *doing*. A "Word of the Year" is about *being*.
It is a theme that flavors your whole year.

## How to Pick One
Don't brainstorm. **Pray.**
Ask the Holy Spirit: *"What grace do I need most right now?"*
- Anxiety? -> **Trust**.
- Anger? -> **Gentleness**.
- Busy? -> **Silence**.

## Saint of the Year
Use our [Saint Generator](/saints) to pick a random saint. Or rather, let them pick *you*.
Research their life. Ask for their intercession daily. You might be surprised why they showed up.
