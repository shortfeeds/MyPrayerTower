On November 1, 1950, Pope Pius XII defined the dogma of the **Assumption**:
> *"The Immaculate Mother of God, the ever Virgin Mary, having completed the course of her earthly life, was assumed body and soul into heavenly glory."*

## Why It Matters
Mary is the **Ark of the New Covenant**.
In the Old Testament, the Ark (which carried the Word of God in stone) could not rot or decay.
Mary carried the Word of God *in flesh*. It would be unfitting for the body that gave life to God to suffer the corruption of the grave.

## Did She Die?
The Church is silent on this.
- **The Dormitionists** believe she "fell asleep" (Dormition) and was assumed without dying.
- **The Mortalists** believe she died (to be like her Son) and was immediately resurrected and assumed.
Both views are permissible.

## It Is Our Future
The Assumption is a preview of *your* future. We believe in the "Resurrection of the Body." One day, our bodies will be reunited with our souls. Mary just got to go first.
