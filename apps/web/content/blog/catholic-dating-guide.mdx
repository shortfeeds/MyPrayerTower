**Rule #1**: Dating is not a hobby. It is an interview for the job of spouse/parent.
If you aren't ready to marry, you aren't ready to date.

## "Hanging Out" vs Courtship
- **Hanging Out**: Ambiguous. No clear intention. Emotional entanglement without commitment.
- **Courtship**: Intentional. "I am interested in seeing if God is calling us to marriage."

## The 3 Questions
1. Do we share the same faith? (Yoke yourself equally).
2. Can I see this person raising my children?
3. Do they make me want to be a saint?
