The **Glorious Mysteries** are the "happy ending" that is actually a new beginning. We pray them on **Wednesdays** and **Sundays**. They lift our eyes from earth to heaven.

## 1. The Resurrection
**The Event**: Christ rises from the dead on the third day.
**The Fruit/Virtue**: **Faith**.
**Meditation**: The tomb is empty. Death has been conquered. No matter how dark my life gets, Sunday is coming.
**Scripture**: *"He is not here; for he has risen, as he said."* (Matthew 28:6)

## 2. The Ascension
**The Event**: Jesus returns to the Father 40 days after Easter.
**The Fruit/Virtue**: **Hope**.
**Meditation**: He goes to prepare a place for me. He takes human nature into the Trinity. I have a Man on the throne of God.
**Scripture**: *"I go to prepare a place for you."* (John 14:2)

## 3. The Descent of the Holy Spirit
**The Event**: Pentecost. The Room fills with wind and fire.
**The Fruit/Virtue**: **Love of God / Zeal for Souls**.
**Meditation**: The frightened Apostles are transformed into bold martyrs. The Spirit turns fear into fire.
**Scripture**: *"And they were all filled with the Holy Spirit and began to speak in other tongues."* (Acts 2:4)

## 4. The Assumption of Mary
**The Event**: Mary is taken up body and soul into heaven at the end of her life.
**The Fruit/Virtue**: **Grace of a Happy Death**.
**Meditation**: She is the "Ark of the New Covenant." The Ark cannot rot in the ground. She follows her Son in all things, including victory over the grave.
**Scripture**: *"Arise, O Lord, impo your resting place; you and the ark of your strength."* (Psalm 132:8)

## 5. The Coronation of Mary
**The Event**: Mary is crowned Queen of Heaven and Earth by the Trinity.
**The Fruit/Virtue**: **Trust in Mary's Intercession**.
**Meditation**: She is not a distant Queen; she is the Queen Mother (Gebirah). In the Davidic kingdom, the King's mother advocated for the people. She advocates for me.
**Scripture**: *"A great sign appeared in heaven: a woman clothed with the sun, with the moon under her feet, and on her head a crown of twelve stars."* (Revelation 12:1)
