Many Catholics settle for Purgatory. *"As long as I make it in!"*
But St. Thérèse says this hurts Jesus. He wants you in Heaven **now**.
Here is how to bypass the purification process.

## 1. Accept Suffering
The "fire" of Purgatory burns away our attachment to sin. Suffering on earth does the exact same thing, but it gains **merit** (Purgatory gains no merit).
When you have a headache, a bad day, or a tragedy, say: *"Jesus, I accept this for love of You."*
You are doing your Purgatory on earth.

## 2. Divine Mercy Sunday
Jesus promised St. Faustina that whoever goes to Confession and receives Communion on the Feast of Divine Mercy receives **complete remission of all sins and punishment**.
It is a total wipe. A plenary indulgence + baptismal reset.
Mark your calendar (Sunday after Easter).

## 3. Forgive Everything
*"Forgive and you will be forgiven."*
If you hold zero grudges, God holds zero debts. It is a straight swap.
