**St. Thérèse of Lisieux** (The Little Flower) realized something depressing: She wasn't strong enough to be a great martyr like St. Joan of Arc. She wasn't smart enough to be a theologian. She fell asleep during prayer.

## The Elevator to Heaven
She read in Scripture: *"Whoever is a little one, let him come to me."*
She decided to engage an elevator to heaven: **The Arms of Jesus.**
Her "Little Way" is simple:
1. Accept your weakness.
2. Trust completely in God's mercy.
3. Do every tiny action (folding laundry, smiling at annoying people) with immense love.

## The Rose Novena
She promised: *"I will spend my heaven doing good on earth. I will let fall a shower of roses."*
Millions of people pray her novena hoping for a sign (a rose).
