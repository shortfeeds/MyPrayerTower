St. Paul was in prison, chained to a Roman soldier. He looked at the soldier's armor and saw a spiritual lesson.

## The Armor of God (Ephesians 6:10-18)
1. **Belt of Truth**: Holds everything together. Without truth (doctrine), your pants fall down.
2. **Breastplate of Righteousness**: Protects the heart. Living a moral life (State of Grace) is your best defense.
3. **Shoes of the Gospel of Peace**: Readiness to move. Evangelization.
4. **Shield of Faith**: Extinguishes the "flaming darts" (lies/temptations) of the Evil One.
5. **Helmet of Salvation**: Protects the head (mind). Hope in Christ keeps you from despair.
6. **Sword of the Spirit**: The Word of God. This is the only offensive weapon. Jesus used Scripture to fight Satan in the desert.

## The Strategy
The devil is a lawyer. He looks for loopholes (unconfessed sin, unforgiveness, occult practices).
**Rule #1 of Warfare**: Close the doors.
- Go to Confession.
- Forgive your enemies.
- Get rid of any New Age objects.
