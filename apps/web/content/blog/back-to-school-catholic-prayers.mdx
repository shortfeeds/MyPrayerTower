The first day of school is exciting and terrifying.
Here are 3 short prayers to say with your kids in the car line.

## 1. Guardian Angel Prayer (The Shield)
> *Angel of God, my guardian dear, to whom God's love commits me here. Ever this day be at my side, to light and guard, to rule and guide. Amen.*
Remind them: "You are never alone in that classroom. Or on the playground."

## 2. Prayer Against Bullying
> *Jesus, King of Peace, give me courage. Help me to be kind to everyone, even the mean kids. But protect me from harm. If I am afraid, remind me that You are with me.*

## 3. Prayer for Focus (St. Thomas Aquinas)
> *Grant me, O Lord my God, a mind to know you, a heart to seek you, wisdom to find you, conduct pleasing to you... and hope embracing you.*

## Pro-Tip: Backpack Blessing
Sprinkle their backpack with Holy Water before they leave. It's a "suit of armor" for their books.
