**Blessed Carlo Acutis**, the millennial "cyber-apostle" (beatified in 2020), had one hobby: cataloging every Eucharistic miracle in history on his website. He knew that for a skeptical modern world, seeing is believing.

Here are 5 miracles where the "veil" was lifted, and the Host physically changed.

## 1. Lanciano, Italy (8th Century)
A monk doubted the Real Presence during Mass. At the words of consecration, the host turned into **flesh** and the wine into **blood**.
**The Science:** In the 1970s, the WHO (World Health Organization) analyzed the samples.
- It is real human flesh.
- It is **myocardium** (heart tissue).
- The blood type is **AB** (same as the Shroud of Turin).
- It is still preserved today, 1,200 years later.

## 2. Buenos Aires, Argentina (1996)
A discarded host was placed in water to dissolve. Instead, it turned into a bloody piece of flesh. Archbishop Bergoglio (now Pope Francis) ordered an investigation.
**The Science:** Dr. Frederick Zugibe (New York cardiologist) analyzed it blindly. He said: *"This is heart muscle from the left ventricle. It is inflamed, meaning the person was beaten severely around the chest. And... the heart was alive when the sample was taken."*

## 3. Santarém, Portugal (13th Century)
A woman stole a host for a sorceress. It began to bleed in her scarf. She hid it in a trunk. In the darkness, the host emitted a bright light that shone through the wood.

## 4. Bolsena-Orvieto, Italy (1263)
A priest doubted. Blood seeped from the host onto the corporal (altar cloth). This miracle led Pope Urban IV to institute the Feast of **Corpus Christi**.

## 5. Sokółka, Poland (2008)
A dropped host was placed in water. A red clot formed.
**The Science:** Pathologists found that the bread and the heart tissue were **interwoven** at a microscopic level. No technology on earth can weave bread and heart muscle together like that.
