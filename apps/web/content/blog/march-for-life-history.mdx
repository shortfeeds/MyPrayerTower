Every January, hundreds of thousands of people descend on Washington D.C. (and other capitals).
They are young. They are joyful. They are freezing.

## The History
The March began in 1974, on the first anniversary of *Roe v. Wade* (Jan 22, 1973), the Supreme Court decision that legalized abortion.
Even though *Roe* fell in 2022 (*Dobbs*), the March continues.
Why? Because abortion still exists. The battle has moved to the states.

## Why We Walk
We don't walk to shout. We walk to **witness**.
We walk for the 60 million lives lost.
We walk for the women who regret their abortions (Silent No More).
We walk because, as Dr. Seuss said: *"A person's a person, no matter how small."*
