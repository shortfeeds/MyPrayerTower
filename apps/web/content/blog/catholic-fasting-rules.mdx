There are only two mandatory days of fasting in the whole year: **Ash Wednesday** and **Good Friday**.

## The Rules (Canon 1251-1252)
1. **Abstinence** (No Meat):
    - Who: Everyone age 14+.
    - When: Ash Wednesday, Good Friday, and ALL Fridays in Lent.
    - Note: Fish, eggs, and dairy are fine. Chicken stock is debated (but usually okay).

2. **Fasting** (Less Food):
    - Who: Adults age 18 to 59.
    - What: One full meal. Two smaller meals that together don't equal a full meal. No snacking between meals.
    - Liquids: Water, coffee, tea, juice are allowed at any time. (Liquid does not break the fast).

3. **Exemptions**:
    - Pregnant/Nursing women.
    - Sick/Diabetic.
    - Manual laborers.
