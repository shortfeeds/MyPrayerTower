The saints faced anxiety and depression too—and they found peace. Here's what they teach us.

---

## Saints Who Struggled

### St. Mother Teresa

Despite her outward joy, Mother Teresa struggled with inner darkness for decades. She kept going—and kept serving.

### St. Josemaria Escriva

He taught: "God, give me the grace to be cheerful." He knew the battle.

### St. Therese of Lisieux

She struggled with "the night of faith"—spiritual dryness and doubt.

---

## Their Wisdom

### 1. Keep Going

The saints didn't stop—they kept moving, praying, serving.

### 2. Offer It Up

They offered their suffering for others. It gave meaning to pain.

### 3. Trust God

In the darkness, they trusted. Even when they couldn't feel.

---

## Practical Advice

### 1. Seek Help

If you're struggling, seek professional help. The saints would too.

### 2. Pray

Prayer changes things—even when it doesn't feel like it.

### 3. Community

Don't isolate. Connect with others.

---

## A Prayer

> *Saint of [whatever you're facing], pray for me. Help me to find peace. Amen.*

---

## Conclusion: You're Not Alone

The saints understand. They've been there. And they want to help.

*Lord, give me the peace the saints found. Amen.*
