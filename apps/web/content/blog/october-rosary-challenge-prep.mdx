October is coming. It is the Super Bowl of Marian devotion.
**October 7** is the Feast of Our Lady of the Rosary (celebrating the naval victory at Lepanto).

## The Challenge
Most of us pray 5 decades (maybe).
This October, try to pray all **20 Mysteries** in one week? Or one day?
- Joyful (Mon/Sat)
- Sorrowful (Tue/Fri)
- Glorious (Wed/Sun)
- Luminous (Thu)

## Why?
St. John Paul II said the Rosary is *"contemplating the face of Christ with Mary."* It is a Bible study on beads.
If your life is chaotic, the Rosary brings **rhythm**. The repetition is like the waves of the ocean; it calms the soul.

## Tools
- Download a Rosary app (like ours!).
- Get a "Scriptural Rosary" book (reads a Bible verse before each Hail Mary).
- Pray while driving (turn off the radio).
