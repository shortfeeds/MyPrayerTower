**Pop Quiz**: Whose conception do we celebrate on December 8?
If you said Jesus, you are wrong (He was conceived March 25).
We celebrate the conception of **Mary** in the womb of her mother, St. Anne.

## The Logic
God cannot touch sin. If Jesus (God) was to live in a human womb for 9 months, that womb had to be pristine.
It couldn't be stained by Original Sin.
So, God applied the merits of Jesus' Cross to Mary **in advance** (preservative redemption).
Think of it like a flu shot: You get the medicine *before* you get sick to prevent infection. Everyone else gets the medicine (Baptism) *after* infection.

## "Full of Grace"
When Gabriel said "Hail, Full of Grace" (Kecharitomene), he used a Greek grammar structure that means "completely, perfectly, and enduringly grace-filled from the past until now."
Mary is the only human (besides Jesus/Adam/Eve) to start life with a full tank of gas.
