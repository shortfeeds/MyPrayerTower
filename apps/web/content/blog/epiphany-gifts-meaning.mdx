The word **Epiphany** means "Manifestation."
Christmas was for the Jews (Shepherds). Epiphany is for the Gentiles (Magi).
Jesus is revealed to the *whole world*.

## The Gifts
1.  **Gold**: For a **King**. (Jesus is the King of Kings).
2.  **Frankincense**: For a **Priest**. (It is incense used in the temple. Jesus is the High Priest).
3.  **Myrrh**: For a **Victim**. (It is an embalming oil for the dead. Jesus was born to die).

## The Names (Legend)
- **Gaspar** (Asian): Brought Frankincense.
- **Melchior** (European): Brought Gold.
- **Balthasar** (African): Brought Myrrh.
Tradition says they represent the three known continents of the ancient world.
