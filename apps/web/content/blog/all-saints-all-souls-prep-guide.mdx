The Triduum of Hallowtide:
1. **Oct 31**: All Hallows Eve (Vigil).
2. **Nov 1**: All Saints Day (Feast).
3. **Nov 2**: All Souls Day (Prayer).

## The Big Deal: Plenary Indulgence (Nov 1-8)
The Church opens the treasury of grace.
From November 1-8, you can gain a **Plenary Indulgence** (full remission of temporal punishment) for a soul in Purgatory, applicable **once per day**.

## How to Get It:
1. Visit a cemetery devoutly and pray for the dead (even mentally).
2. Go to Confession (within 20 days).
3. Receive Holy Communion.
4. Pray for the Pope (Our Father + Hail Mary).
5. Be detached from all sin.

It is a "Get Out of Jail Free" card you can give to your grandma. Don't waste it.
