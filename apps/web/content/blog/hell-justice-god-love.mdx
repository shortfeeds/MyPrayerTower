The most common objection to Christianity: *"I can't believe in a God who sends people to burn forever."*

## The Locked Door
The Church teaches that God sends **no one** to hell. People send themselves.
Hell is the definitive self-exclusion from communion with God.
If you spend your whole life saying to God, *"Leave me alone, I don't want your rules, I don't want your mercy,"* — at death, God respects your choice. He says: *"Thy will be done."*

## Why Fire?
The "fire" of hell might be the **Love of God**.
- To a soul that loves God, His presence is warmth and light (Heaven).
- To a soul that hates God, His presence is burning and torture (Hell).
It is the same Fire, experienced differently.

## Is It Empty?
We hope so (Hans Urs von Balthasar). But Jesus warns us repeatedly about "wailing and gnashing of teeth." We cannot presume everyone is saved. That is the sin of presumption.
