A "Litany" is a prayer of repetition. It is like a love song where the chorus repeats. The **Litany of the Sacred Heart** has 33 titles for Jesus — one for each year of His life on earth.

It is often prayed on the Feast of the Sacred Heart and on First Fridays.

## Key Invocations Explained

**"Heart of Jesus, Son of the Eternal Father"**
We start by affirming His divinity. This human heart beating in a human chest is the Heart of God.

**"Heart of Jesus, formed by the Holy Spirit in the womb of the Virgin Mother"**
We honor the Incarnation. The Holy Spirit knit this heart together in Mary's womb.

**"Heart of Jesus, burning furnace of charity"**
Fire symbolizes divinity and intensity. His love is not lukewarm; it consumes.

**"Heart of Jesus, patient and most merciful"**
Think of how patient He was with the Apostles. Think of how patient He is with you.

**"Heart of Jesus, propitiation for our sins"**
"Propitiation" means *atonement*. This Heart paid the debt we could not pay.

**"Heart of Jesus, salvation of those who trust in Thee"**
Trust is the key that unlocks the Heart. "Jesus, I trust in You."

## Indulgence
A partial indulgence is granted to the faithful who recite this litany. A **plenary indulgence** is granted if it is publicly recited on the Feast of the Sacred Heart.
