"I don't need *Natural Family Planning*; I need something that works."

This is the biggest myth about NFP — that it is the old "Rhythm Method" (guessing based on a calendar). Modern NFP is **fertility awareness based on biomarkers**. It is science, not guesswork.

## How It Works
A woman's body gives clear signs when she is fertile (about 5-7 days per cycle). NFP teaches couples to read these signs.
- **To avoid pregnancy**: Abstain during the fertile window.
- **To achieve pregnancy**: Target the fertile window.

## The Methods
1. **Marquette Method**: Uses a Clearblue fertility monitor to test urine hormones. Very objective (98-99% effective). Great for postpartum.
2. **Creighton Model**: Tracks cervical mucus observations. Very detailed. Great for diagnosing health issues (NaproTechnology).
3. **Sympto-Thermal**: Tracks mucus + basal body temperature.

## Why Not Contraception?
Contraception says: *"I give myself to you, but NOT my fertility."* It holds a part of the person back.
NFP says: *"I give all of myself to you. We will abstain for a few days because we invite God into our family planning, but I am not altering my body to be sterile."*
It demands **self-control**, communication, and respect.

## The Benefits
- **No side effects**: No artificial hormones, blood clot risks, or libido kill.
- **Shared responsibility**: It is not just the woman's job; the husband must participate in the charting and abstinence.
- **Better communication**: You have to talk about your sex life and your family goals every month.

## FAQ

### Is it hard?
It has a learning curve. Taking a class is essential.

### Is it "Catholic Contraception"?
No. Contraception *breaks* the act (sterilizing it). NFP *respects* the act by refraining from it during fertile times. The intention to avoid pregnancy may be the same, but the *means* matter. (Just as killing grandma and waiting for grandma to die naturally both result in death, but one is murder and one is life).
