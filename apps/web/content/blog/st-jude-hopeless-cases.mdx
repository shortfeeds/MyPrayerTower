**St. Jude Thaddeus** was one of the 12 Apostles (and Jesus' cousin).
He is often depicted with a flame on his head (Pentecost) and holding a medal of Christ (The Mandylion).

## Why "Hopeless"?
For centuries, people confused him with **Judas Iscariot** (the traitor). So, nobody prayed to him. He was the "forgotten" apostle.
Tradition says that because he was ignored for so long, he is eager to help anyone who asks him, especially in desperate situations where everyone else has failed.

## The Prayer
> *St. Jude, helper of the hopeless, pray for me in my distress. Make use, I implore you, of that particular privilege given to you, to bring visible and speedy help where help is almost despaired of.*
