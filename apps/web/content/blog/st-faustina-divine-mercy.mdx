**St. Faustina** (1905-1938) was an uneducated nun who wrote the most important book of the 20th century: *The Diary: Divine Mercy in My Soul*.

## The Image
Jesus asked her to paint an image of Him with rays of red (Blood) and pale (Water) flowing from His heart.
The signature: **Jesus, I Trust in You.**

## The Message
God is Mercy itself.
> *"The greater the sinner, the greater the right he has to My mercy."*
Jesus told her that this devotion was preparation for His Second Coming: *"You will prepare the world for My final coming."*

## The 3 O'Clock Hour
Jesus asked us to immerse ourselves in His Passion at 3:00 PM (the hour He died). Even for a moment.
*"In this hour, I will refuse nothing to the soul that makes a request of Me in virtue of My Passion."*
