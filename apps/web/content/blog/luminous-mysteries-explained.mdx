Traditionally, the Rosary jumped from Jesus being a 12-year-old (Finding in the Temple) to Jesus sweating blood (Agony in the Garden).
It skipped His entire adult life!

## The Missing Link
St. John Paul II realized this gap. In his apostolic letter *Rosarium Virginis Mariae*, he proposed the **Luminous Mysteries** (Mysteries of Light) to bridge the gap.
They focus on Christ's public ministry:
1. **The Baptism in the Jordan** (His Identity revealed).
2. **The Wedding at Cana** (His first miracle / Mary's intercession).
3. **The Proclamation of the Kingdom** (His preaching/healing).
4. **The Transfiguration** (His glory revealed).
5. **The Institution of the Eucharist** (His gift of Himself).

## Why "Luminous"?
Because Jesus is the "Light of the World." These mysteries show Him revealing who He really is.
