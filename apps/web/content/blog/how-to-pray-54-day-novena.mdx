It is called the "Irresistible Novena."
It consists of praying 3 rosaries (15 decades) daily (or at least 5 decades) for **54 consecutive days**.

## The Structure
- **27 Days of Petition**: Asking for the favor.
- **27 Days of Thanksgiving**: Thanking God for the favor (even if you haven't received it yet).

## The Origin (Fortuna Agrelli)
In 1884, a young girl named Fortuna Agrelli was dying in Naples. She and her family prayed desperately. Mary appeared to her as the **Queen of the Holy Rosary of Pompeii**.
Mary said: *"Whoever desires to obtain favors from me should make three novenas of the prayers of the Rosary, and three novenas in thanksgiving."*
(A novena is 9 days. 3 x 9 = 27. 27 + 27 = 54).
Fortuna was instantly cured.

## Why Do It?
It is a test of endurance. It proves you are serious. It is spiritual bootcamp.
Thousands of people attest to miracles—jobs found, cancers cured, marriages saved—after completing the 54 days.
