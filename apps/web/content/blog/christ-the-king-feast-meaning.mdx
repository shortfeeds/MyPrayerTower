In 1925, the world was messy.
Mussolini was rising in Italy. Communism was spreading in Russia. Mexico was persecuting Catholics (Viva Cristo Rey!).
Secular governments were saying: *"Religion is private. Keep it in the sacristy. Don't let it influence laws or culture."*

Pope Pius XI said: **No.**
He established the Feast of **Christ the King** to remind the world that Jesus is not just the King of "our hearts." He is the King of the Universe.
- He has authority over nations.
- He has authority over economies.
- He has authority over history.

## What It Means for You
If Christ is King, then He isn't just a "hobby." You can't be a Catholic on Sunday and a secularist on Monday. He claims **everything**.
