**Advent is not Christmas.**
If you celebrate Christmas starting Nov 1, you will be sick of it by Dec 25.
The Church saves the best for last.

## 1. Don't Decorate Yet
Wait. Put up the tree, but leave it bare. Add ornaments slowly.
Put the Baby Jesus in a drawer until Christmas Eve. Let the manger be empty.
This builds **anticipation**.

## 2. Silence the Carols
Listen to Advent music (ex: "O Come O Come Emmanuel," "creator of the Stars of Night"). Save "Jingle Bells" for the 12 Days of Christmas (Dec 25 - Jan 6).

## 3. The O Antiphons (Dec 17-23)
In the final week, the Church prays the ancient "O Antiphons" (O Wisdom, O Adonai, O Root of Jesse).
Each one is a title of Christ.
Fun Fact: If you take the first letter of each Latin title in reverse order, it spells **ERO CRAS** ("Tomorrow, I will be there").
