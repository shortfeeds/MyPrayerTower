On **September 14**, the Church exalts (lifts up) the Cross.
To the Romans, the cross was a symbol of shame. To us, it is a trophy.
*"We adore You, O Christ, and we praise You, because by Your Holy Cross You have redeemed the world."*

## The History (326 AD)
**St. Helena** (mother of Emperor Constantine) went to Jerusalem to find the holy sites.
Tradition says she found three crosses buried near Golgotha. How did she know which one was Jesus'?
The Bishop of Jerusalem suggested a test. They brought a dying woman to touch the crosses.
- Cross #1: Nothing happened.
- Cross #2: Nothing happened.
- Cross #3: She was instantly healed.

## Why "Exalt"?
Because the Cross is the **Tree of Life**.
In Eden, Adam ate from a tree and brought death. On Calvary, the New Adam (Jesus) hung on a tree and brought life.
The Cross is the key that unlocks heaven.
