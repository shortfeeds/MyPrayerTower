Of all the promises Jesus made to St. Margaret Mary, the **12th Promise** (The Great Promise) is the most specific and the most powerful.

> *"I promise you in the excessive mercy of my Heart that my all-powerful love will grant to all those who shall receive Communion on the First Friday of nine consecutive months the grace of final penitence."*

In plain English: **You will not die without the Sacraments. You will make it to heaven.**

## The Requirements

### 1. Mass and Communion
You must attend Mass and receive Holy Communion on the **First Friday** of the month.
*Note*: If you are in a state of mortal sin, you must go to Confession first.

### 2. Nine Consecutive Months
You cannot skip a month. If you get sick or forget in Month 5, you have to start over at Month 1.
*Why?* It tests perseverance and fidelity.

### 3. Intention of Reparation
You must offer your Holy Communion with the intention of **making reparation** to the Sacred Heart for all the insults, sacrileges, and indifference He receives in the Eucharist. You are loving Him for those who don't.

## Why Fridays?
Friday is the day Jesus died. It is the day His heart was pierced. The Church has always marked Friday as a day of penance.

## Checklist for Success
- [ ] Set a recurring alarm in your phone for the *Thursday before* First Friday.
- [ ] Check Mass times (many parishes have evening Masses for workers).
- [ ] Go to Confession the week before.
