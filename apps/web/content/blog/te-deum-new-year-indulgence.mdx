The Church offers a special gift on December 31.
A **Plenary Indulgence** is granted to the faithful who recite or sing the hymn *Te Deum* ("Thee, O God, we praise") in thanksgiving for the past year.

## Why Thank God for a Bad Year?
Even if you had a terrible year, you are still alive. You still have grace.
St. Paul says: *"Give thanks in all circumstances."*
Gratitude is the only way to close the door on the past.

## The Conditions
1. Recite the *Te Deum* publicly (in a church) or privately.
2. Confession + Communion.
3. Pray for the Pope.
4. Detachment from sin.
