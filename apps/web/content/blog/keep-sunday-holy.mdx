Sunday is the day set aside for God—but with busy schedules, it can feel impossible to keep it holy.

Here's how.

---

## The Challenge

### Busy Lives

Work, family, responsibilities—the weekend fills up fast.

### The Command

"Remember the Sabbath day, to keep it holy" (Exodus 20:8).

---

## The Solution

### 1. Plan Ahead

Schedule Sunday like any other important appointment. Protect it.

### 2. Prioritize Mass

Make Mass the first priority. Everything else fits around it.

### 3. Simplify

You don't need a "perfect" Sunday. Keep it simple.

### 4. Rest

Actually rest. Don't work. Don't do chores.

---

## A Prayer

> *Lord, help me to keep Sunday holy. Give me the grace to prioritize Your day. Amen.*

---

## Conclusion: It's Worth It

Sunday rest is worth it—for you, your family, your soul.

*Lord, make Sunday a day of rest and worship. Amen.*
