Purgatory is misunderstood. It is not Hell-Lite. It is not a place where you "might" go to Heaven.
**Everyone in Purgatory is saved.** They are already on the bus to heaven; they just need to wipe their muddy boots before entering the King's throne room.

## The Logic
Revelation 21:27 says: *"Nothing unclean shall enter heaven."*
If you die with hate in your heart, or addiction to sin, you CANNOT enter heaven. You would hate it there. You need to be purified of that attachment to sin. That process is Purgatory.

## The Scripture

### 2 Maccabees 12:46
*"It is a holy and wholesome thought to pray for the dead, that they may be loosed from sins."*
The Jews prayed for the dead. Why? If they are in Heaven, they don't need prayer. If they are in Hell, prayer doesn't help. There must be a third state.
*(Note: Luther removed Maccabees from his Bible precisely because of this verse).*

### 1 Corinthians 3:15
Paul speaks of a man whose work is burned up on Judgment Day: *"He will be saved, but only as through fire."*
Saved through fire. That is Purgatory.

### Matthew 12:32
Jesus says sins against the Holy Spirit will not be forgiven *"in this age or in the age to come."* This implies that *some* sins can be forgiven in the age to come.
