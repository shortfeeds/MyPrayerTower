When someone struggles mentally or spiritually, it can be hard to know: Is it a medical issue or a spiritual one?

Let's clarify.

---

## The Reality of Both

### Mental Health Issues Are Real

Depression, anxiety, bipolar, OCD—these are real medical conditions. They often require professional treatment.

### Spiritual Warfare Is Real

Demons are real. Spiritual attack is real. But it's less common than mental health issues.

---

## The Distinction

### Symptoms of Mental Illness

- Persistent sadness or worry
- Changes in sleep, appetite, energy
- Difficulty functioning
- Symptoms consistent over time

### Symptoms of Spiritual Attack

- Sudden, dramatic change
- Physical manifestations
- Resistance to prayer/Church
- Specific targeting

---

## What to Do

### 1. Seek Professional Help

If it's a mental health issue, get help. Therapy and medication are valid.

### 2. Pray

Prayer helps either way. Don't choose between prayer and treatment.

### 3. Discern

Talk to a priest and a doctor. Both can help.

---

## Conclusion: Both Matter

You can pursue mental health treatment AND spiritual warfare at the same time. They're not mutually exclusive.
