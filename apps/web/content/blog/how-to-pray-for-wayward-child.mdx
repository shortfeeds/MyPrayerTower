The statistics are grim. 50% of young Catholics leave the faith.
If you are a parent, this is your worst nightmare. You feel guilty. *"Where did we go wrong?"*

Stop. Guilt helps no one. God loves your child more than you do.

## 1. The Green Martyrdom (Fasting)
Prayer is good. Prayer + Fasting is atomic.
Jesus said some demons are only cast out by prayer and fasting (Mark 9:29). Offer your hunger pangs for your child's conversion.

## 2. The Rosary (The Cord)
Imagine the Rosary as a lasso. Every Hail Mary wraps around your child's heart, gently pulling them back. They may run, but the Virgin Mary is holding the other end of the rope.

## 3. Don't Be Annoying
You cannot argue someone into the Kingdom.
- **Do**: Love them. Feed them. Listen to them. Be joyful.
- **Don't**: Leave tracts on their pillow. Send passive-aggressive texts. Critique their lifestyle at Thanksgiving.
St. Francis de Sales said: *"A spoonful of honey attracts more flies than a barrel of vinegar."*

## 4. Surrender (The hardest part)
You can't save them. Only Jesus can.
Pray the **Surrender Novena**: *"O Jesus, I surrender myself to you, take care of everything."*
