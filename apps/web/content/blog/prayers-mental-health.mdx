Mental health struggles are real—and prayer helps. Here are prayers for those struggling.

---

## A Prayer for Depression

> *Lord, I am in darkness. Lift me up. Give me hope. Help me to see the light. Amen.*

---

## A Prayer for Anxiety

> *Lord, I am anxious about [situation]. Help me to trust You. Give me peace. Amen.*

---

## A Prayer for Those Who Can't Get Out of Bed

> *Lord, I can't face today. Give me strength for just this moment. Carry me. Amen.*

---

## A Prayer for Those Feeling Alone

> *Lord, I feel alone. But I know You are with me. Never leave me. Amen.*

---

## A Prayer for the Caregivers

> *Lord, give me strength to care for my loved one. Help me to be patient. Give me Your love. Amen.*

---

## Conclusion: God Understands

God understands our struggles. He's with us in them.

*Lord, be with all who struggle with mental health. Give them hope. Amen.*
