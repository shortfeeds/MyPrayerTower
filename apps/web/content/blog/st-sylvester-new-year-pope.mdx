In many countries (Germany, Israel, Poland), New Year's Eve is called **Silvester**.
Who was he?
**Pope St. Sylvester I** reigned during a massive turning point: The conversion of Emperor Constantine.
He oversaw the Council of Nicaea (which gave us the Creed).
He built the first great basilicas in Rome (St. John Lateran, St. Peter's).

## The Transition
He represents the shift from the "Church of the Martyrs" (underground) to the "Church of the Empire" (public).
He is the saint of **new eras**.
If you are facing a major life transition this year, ask St. Sylvester for guidance.
