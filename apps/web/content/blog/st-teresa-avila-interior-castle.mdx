**St. Teresa of Avila** (1515-1582) was the first female Doctor of the Church.
She was a mystic, but she was grounded.
Famous Quote: *"God is found among the pots and pans."*

## The Reform
The Carmelite order had become relaxed. Nuns had servants, pets, and visitors. Teresa wanted to return to the primitive rule of poverty and prayer. She faced investigation by the Inquisition, but she won.

## The Interior Castle
She visualized the soul as a diamond castle containing many rooms (mansions). At the very center is the King (God).
- **Mansion 1-3 (Active Prayer)**: We fight sin, practice humility, and start to pray.
- **Mansion 4-7 (Passive Prayer)**: God takes over. He gives consolations, spiritual betrothal, and finally **Spiritual Marriage** (Union).

## Her Bookmark Prayer
> *Let nothing disturb you, Let nothing frighten you, All things are passing away: God never changes. Patience obtains all things. Whoever has God lacks nothing; God alone suffices.*
