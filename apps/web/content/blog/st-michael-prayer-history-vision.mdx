On October 13, 1884, Pope Leo XIII finished Mass and suddenly turned pale. He stood frozen for several minutes. He then ran to his office and wrote the **St. Michael Prayer**.

He ordered it to be recited after every Low Mass in the world.

## The Vision
Cardinals asked him what happened. He reportedly heard a conversation between God and Satan near the tabernacle.
**Satan**: *"I can destroy your Church."*
**God**: *"You can? Then go ahead and do so."*
**Satan**: *"I need more time and more power."*
**God**: *"How much time? How much power?"*
**Satan**: *"75 to 100 years, and a greater power over those who will give themselves over to my service."*
**God**: *"You have the time, you have the power. Do with them what you will."*

Leo realized the 20th century would be a time of unprecedented evil (Communism, Nazism, Abortion, War). He gave us the weapon.

## The Text
> *St. Michael the Archangel, defend us in battle. Be our protection against the wickedness and snares of the devil. May God rebuke him we humbly pray, and do thou, O Prince of the Heavenly Host, by the power of God, cast into hell Satan and all the evil spirits who prowl about the world seeking the ruin of souls. Amen.*

(Note: The original prayer Leo wrote was actually much longer and is used in Exorcisms).
