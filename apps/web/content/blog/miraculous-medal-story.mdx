It was originally called the "Medal of the Immaculate Conception."
But so many miracles happened to people who wore it that the people renamed it **The Miraculous Medal**.

## The Vision
**St. Catherine Labouré** was a Sister of Charity in Paris (Rue du Bac). Mary appeared to her standing on a globe, crushing a serpent. Rays of light streamed from her hands.
The Prayer: *"O Mary, conceived without sin, pray for us who have recourse to thee."*

## The Symbols (Back of Medal)
- **M + Cross**: Mary's connection to Jesus' suffering.
- **Two Hearts**: Sacred Heart (Jesus) and Immaculate Heart (Mary).
- **12 Stars**: The Woman of Revelation 12 / The Apostles.

## The Secret
Catherine told no one but her confessor. She lived her whole life in anonymity, taking care of chickens and elderly men. It wasn't until she died (46 years later) that her sisters found out *she* was the visionary.
Her body is incorrupt in Paris today.
