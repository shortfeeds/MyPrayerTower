**Shrove Tuesday** (the day before Ash Wednesday) has two vibes:
1. **Party**: Mardi Gras ("Fat Tuesday"). Eating up all the rich foods (butter, eggs, sugar) before the Lenten fast. That is why we make **Pancakes**.
2. **Penance**: "Shrove" comes from the word "Shrive" (to confess).
Historically, Christians went to Confession on this day to start Lent with a clean soul.

## The Balance
Have your pancakes. Enjoy the party.
But remember: The party ends at midnight.
Ash Wednesday is coming.
