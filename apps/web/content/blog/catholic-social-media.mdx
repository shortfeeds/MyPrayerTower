Social media can be a minefield for Catholics. Here's how to navigate it with grace.

---

## The Challenge

### Division

Social media divides. People attack. Conflict is constant.

### Temptation

It's easy to argue, judge, and get angry.

---

## The Solution

### 1. Don't Engage

When someone's attacking, don't reply. It's not worth it.

### 2. Speak Truth in Love

If you do engage, be gentle, truthful, and kind.

### 3. Limit Exposure

Don't spend hours scrolling. Set limits.

### 4. Use It for Good

Share good content—faith, inspiration, positivity.

---

## A Prayer Before Posting

> *Lord, guide my words and actions online. Help me to bring light, not heat. Amen.*

---

## Conclusion: Be the Light

Use social media for good.

*Lord, help me to be a light online. Amen.*

---
