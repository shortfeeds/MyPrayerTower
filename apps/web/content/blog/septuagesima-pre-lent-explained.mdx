Lent is hard. You don't just sprint into a marathon. You need to stretch first.
That is **Septuagesima** (roughly 70 days before Easter).

## The "Goodbye to Alleluia"
In the traditional calendar, the "Alleluia" is buried three Sundays before Ash Wednesday.
The priest wears purple. The Gloria is omitted.
It is a "soft launch" of Lent. It gives you 2.5 weeks to eat the ice cream, figure out your penances, and get your mind ready for the desert.

## Bringing it Back
Even if your parish doesn't celebrate it, you can. Start weaning yourself off Netflix. Start praying the Sorrowful Mysteries. Ease into the fast.
