The Works of Mercy are acts of charity—14 ways to serve others. Let's explore each.

---

## The Corporal Works of Mercy

(Serving the body)

1. **Feed the hungry**
2. **Give drink to the thirsty**
3. **Clothe the naked**
4. **Shelter the homeless**
5. **Visit the imprisoned**
6. **Bury the dead**
7. **Care for the sick**

---

## The Spiritual Works of Mercy

(Serving the soul)

1. **Instruct the ignorant**
2. **Counsel the doubtful**
3. **Admonish the sinners**
4. **Bear patiently with those who wrong us**
5. **Forgive injuries**
6. **Pray for the living and the dead**

---

## How to Practice Them

### Daily

Look for opportunities in your daily life.

### Intentional

Volunteer at a charity, hospital, or prison.

### In Family

Teach your children to practice mercy.

---

## Conclusion: Live Them Out

The Works of Mercy aren't just a list—they're a way of life.
