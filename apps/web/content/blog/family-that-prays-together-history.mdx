You've seen it on billboards. You've heard it in homilies.
**"The Family That Prays Together Stays Together."**

## The Rosary Priest
It was coined by **Fr. Patrick Peyton** (1909-1992), an Irish immagrant to the U.S. who was miraculously healed of tuberculosis through the Rosary.
He dedicated his life to promoting the Family Rosary.
He was a media genius. He got Hollywood stars (Bing Crosby, Grace Kelly, Frank Sinatra) to appear on his radio shows to promote prayer.

## The Logic
Why does it work?
1. **Vulnerability**: You can't stay angry at your spouse while kneeling next to them praying to God.
2. **Shared Vision**: It aligns the family's "compass" to True North (Heaven).
3. **Peace**: It invites the Prince of Peace into the living room.
