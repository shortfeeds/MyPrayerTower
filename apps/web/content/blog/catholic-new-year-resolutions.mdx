80% of resolutions fail by February.
Why? Because they rely on willpower instead of grace.
This year, set **Micro-Goals**:

## 1. The 1% Rule (15 mins of Prayer)
Don't say "I will pray more." Be specific. "I will pray 15 minutes before I check my phone."
(15 mins = 1% of your day).

## 2. Monthly Confession
Put it on the calendar now. First Saturday of every month.

## 3. Read the Bible (5 mins)
Don't try to read everything. Read one chapter of the Gospels a day.

## 4. Friday Meatless Fast
Bring back the old school tradition. It builds discipline.

## 5. Tithing (Start Small)
Give 1% more to charity than you did last year.
