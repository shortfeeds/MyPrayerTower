Want to make a difference? Here's how to volunteer effectively as a Catholic.

---

## The Call to Service

### We All Have Gifts

Each of us has gifts to share—time, talent, treasure.

### The Call

Serving others is part of our calling—following Christ's example.

---

## How to Volunteer Effectively

### 1. Identify Your Gifts

What are you good at? What do you enjoy? Use those.

### 2. Choose Wisely

Don't spread yourself thin. Choose one or two commitments.

### 3. Commit

Once you commit, follow through. Don't flake.

---

## Where to Serve

- Your parish
- Catholic charities
- Local organizations
- Pro-life groups

---

## Conclusion: Serve Intentionally

Don't just "volunteer"—serve with purpose.

*Lord, show me where to serve. Give me the gift to do it well. Amen.*
