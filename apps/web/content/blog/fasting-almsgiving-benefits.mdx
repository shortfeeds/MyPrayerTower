Fasting and almsgiving are core Catholic practices. But why do we do them? Let's explore.

---

## What Is Fasting?

### The Practice

Fasting is abstaining—limiting food, drink, or other pleasures.

### The Purpose

Fasting:
- Trains the soul
- Creates space for God
- Expresses repentance

---

## What Is Almsgiving?

### The Practice

Almsgiving is giving to the poor—money, time, resources.

### The Purpose

Almsgiving:
- Helps the poor
- Detaches from material things
- Shows God's love

---

## The Spiritual Benefits

### 1. Freedom

Fasting and giving free us from attachments.

### 2. Focus

They help us focus on what matters—God.

### 3. Mercy

They make us more merciful—to others, by God's grace.

---

## Conclusion: Do Them

Fasting and almsgiving are ancient practices. They still work.

*Lord, teach me to fast and give. Amen.*
