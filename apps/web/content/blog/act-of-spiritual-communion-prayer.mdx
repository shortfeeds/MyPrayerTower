During the 2020 lockdowns, millions of Catholics learned the value of **Spiritual Communion**. But this isn't just a "Plan B" for pandemics. It is a daily habit of the saints.

**What is it?**
It is a burning desire to receive Jesus in the Eucharist, even when you cannot physically do so. St. Thomas Aquinas taught that the *desire* alone can bring you similar graces to the actual Sacrament.

## The Prayer (St. Alphonsus Liguori)
Memorize this. Use it when you watch Mass on TV, when you drive past a church, or when you wake up in the middle of the night.

> *My Jesus,*
> *I believe that You are present in the Most Holy Sacrament.*
> *I love You above all things, and I desire to receive You into my soul.*
> *Since I cannot at this moment receive You sacramentally,*
> *come at least spiritually into my heart.*
> *I embrace You as if You were already there and unite myself wholly to You.*
> *Never permit me to be separated from You.*
> *Amen.*

## Why Do It?
St. Leonard of Port Maurice said: *"If you practice the holy exercise of Spiritual Communion... within a month you will see your heart completely changed."*
It keeps your "Eucharistic hunger" alive. It prevents your soul from getting cold between Sundays.
