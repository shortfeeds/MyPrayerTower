Every October, the debate rages in mom groups.
*"Halloween glorifies Satan!"* vs *"It's just candy and fun!"*
Here is the balanced Catholic view.

## The Danger Zone
Yes, modern culture has twisted Halloween.
- **Gore/Murder**: Dressing as serial killers or glorifying torture is not healthy. It desensitizes us to violence.
- **Occult**: Ouija boards, séances, and spelling-casting are gravely sinful. They open doors to demons. Avoid them at all costs.

## The Safe Zone
Dressing up as Spiderman, a princess, or a hot dog is innocent fun.
Mocking the devil is a Catholic tradition.
St. Thomas More said: *"The devil, the spirit of pride, cannot endure to be mocked."*

## The Middle Ground (All Saints Party)
Many parishes host an "All Saints Party" on Oct 31 or Nov 1. Kids dress as saints.
**Verdict**: You can celebrate Halloween, but keep it wholesome. Focus on the Light, not the Darkness.
