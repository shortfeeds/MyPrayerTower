Want to succeed professionally—but stay humble? Here's how.

---

## The Tension

### Ambition Is Good

God wants us to excel, to use our gifts, to build.

### Humility Is Essential

But we must stay humble—remembering it's all from God.

---

## The Balance

### 1. Work Hard

Give your best effort—whatever you do, do it for the Lord.

### 2. Give Credit

When you succeed, give credit to others. To God.

### 3. Learn

Stay teachable. Never stop learning.

### 4. Serve

Use your success to serve—help others, give back.

---

## A Prayer

> *Lord, help me to be ambitious for Your glory and humble in my heart. Amen.*

---

## Conclusion: It's Both

You can be ambitious AND humble. Let God guide both.

*Lord, direct my career. Amen.*
