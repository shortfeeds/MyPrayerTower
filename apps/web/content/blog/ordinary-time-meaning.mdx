"Ordinary" doesn't mean boring.
It comes from the Latin *ordinalis* (numbered). We count the weeks (1st Sunday, 2nd Sunday...).

## The Color Green
The liturgical color is **Green**. It represents:
- **Hope**: Like spring shoots.
- **Growth**: This is the season to grow in your daily spiritual life.
During High Feasts (Christmas/Easter), we sprint.
During Ordinary Time, we run the marathon. It is where the real work happens.
