John the Baptist was confused too. He tried to stop Him: *"I need to be baptized by you, and do you come to me?"*
Jesus replied: *"Let it be so now, for thus it is fitting for us to fulfill all righteousness."*

## 3 Reasons
1. **Solidarity**: He got in line with sinners not because He *was* one, but to show He stands *with* us. He took our place.
2. **Sanctification**: Water cleanses us. But Jesus cleansed the water. By entering the Jordan, He made all water capable of being holy (Sacrament of Baptism).
3. **The Trinity**: It is the first time all three Persons appear together implicitly. The Voice (Father), the Dove (Spirit), the Beloved Son.
