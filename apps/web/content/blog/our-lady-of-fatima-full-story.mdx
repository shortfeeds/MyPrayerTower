On **May 13, 1917**, in a small village in Portugal, three illiterate children (Lucia, Francisco, and Jacinta) saw a "Lady dressed in white, shining brighter than the sun."

What followed was a series of monthly apparitions that culminated in the most witnessed public miracle in biblical history — the **Miracle of the Sun** — and a message that accurately predicted World War II and the rise of Russia.

> *"Pray the Rosary every day to obtain peace for the world and the end of the war."*

## The Message: Prayer & Penance
Mary didn't come to chat. She came with a warning: Humanity was offending God, and if they didn't stop, worse wars would come.
Her solution was simple:
1. **Pray the Rosary** daily.
2. **Offer sacrifices** for sinners.
3. **Consecrate Russia** to her Immaculate Heart.

## The Miracle of the Sun (Oct 13, 1917)
Mary promised a miracle so "all might believe." On the final apparition, 70,000 people (including atheists, journalists, and skeptics) gathered in the rain.
Suddenly, the clouds parted. The sun appeared as a spinning disc of opaque silver. It danced in the sky, cast multi-colored lights across the earth, and then hurtled toward the crowd as if to crush them. The ground, which had been muddy, was instantly dry.
It was reported in secular newspapers the next day.

## The Three Secrets

### 1. A Vision of Hell
She showed the children a terrifying sea of fire where the souls of poor sinners go. *“To save them, God wishes to establish in the world devotion to my Immaculate Heart.”*

### 2. The Prophecy of War
She predicted that WWI would end, but if people didn't stop offending God, a worse one (WWII) would break out during the reign of Pius XI. She also predicted that Russia would "spread her errors throughout the world" (Communism).

### 3. The Bishop in White
A vision of a Pope walking through a ruined city and being killed by soldiers. This is widely believed to refer to the assassination attempt on **St. John Paul II** on May 13, 1981 (the feast of Fatima). He survived and credited Our Lady of Fatima with guiding the bullet.

## The Fatima Prayer
She taught the children to add this prayer after every decade of the Rosary:
> *O my Jesus, forgive us our sins, save us from the fires of hell. Lead all souls to Heaven, especially those in most need of Thy mercy.*
