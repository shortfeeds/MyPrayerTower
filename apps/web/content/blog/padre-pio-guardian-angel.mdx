**St. Pio of Pietrelcina** (1887-1968) lived with one foot in heaven and one on earth.
He bore the stigmata (wounds of Christ) for 50 years. But his relationship with angels was uniquely charming.

## "Send Him to Me"
Padre Pio was busy. He heard confessions for 15 hours a day. He couldn't answer letters.
So he told his spiritual children:
> *"If you need me, send your Guardian Angel to me."*

One night, a spiritual son (Cecil) was in a car accident. His friend was injured. Cecil remembered Pio's words and mentally said: *"Guardian Angel, go tell Padre Pio to help us."*
The next day, he arrived at the friary. Padre Pio saw him and said: *"Boy, didn't your angel keep me up all night! You don't leave me alone even when I'm sleeping!"*

## Translators
Padre Pio received letters from all over the world in languages he didn't speak (German, English, French). But he read them instantly.
When asked how, he smiled: *"My angel translates for me."*

## The Battle
He also knew the dark side. Demons would physically beat him in his cell at night, leaving bruises. He called the devil "Bluebeard." But he was never afraid, because he knew St. Michael was stronger.
