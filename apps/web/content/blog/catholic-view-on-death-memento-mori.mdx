The world pretends death doesn't exist. We hide it in hospitals. We put makeup on corpses.
The Church puts a skeleton on the altar.

## Memento Mori
"Remember you will die."
It is not meant to scare you. It is meant to **focus** you.
If you knew you had 1 week to live, would you waste time scrolling TikTok? Or would you forgive your brother, confess your sins, and hug your kids?
Death clarifies life.

## The Door (Dies Natalis)
The early Christians called the day of death *Dies Natalis* (Birthday).
They understood that this life is the womb. It is dark, cramped, and temporary. Death is the birth canal into the real world.

## St. Joseph, Patron of the Dying
We pray to St. Joseph for a "Happy Death" because he died in the arms of Jesus and Mary. That is the goal: To die in the state of grace, surrounded by love.
