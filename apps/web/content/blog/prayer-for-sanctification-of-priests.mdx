In 2002, St. John Paul II designated the **Feast of the Sacred Heart** as the **World Day of Prayer for the Sanctification of Priests**.

Why? Because the priest is *Alter Christus* (Another Christ). His heart must be conformed to the Sacred Heart.
But priests are human. They get tired, lonely, and tempted. The devil attacks them harder because *"if you strike the shepherd, the sheep scatter"* (Zechariah 13:7).

## How to Pray for Your Priest

### 1. Offer a Holy Hour
Go to Adoration specifically for him. Pray that he falls in love with the Eucharist daily.

### 2. Fast for Him
Pick a day (Friday is good) to fast from a meal or a comfort, offering the suffering for his purity and strength.

### 3. A Prayer for Priests (by St. Thérèse of Lisieux)
> *O Jesus, I pray for your faithful and fervent priests; for your unfaithful and tepid priests; for your priests laboring at home or abroad in distant mission fields; for your tempted priests; for your lonely and desolate priests; for your young priests; for your dying priests; for the souls of your priests in Purgatory. But above all, I recommend to you the priests dearest to me: the priest who baptized me; the priests who absolved me from my sins...*

### 4. Say "Thank You"
Send a card. Tell him a specific homily that helped you. Priests hear complaints all day; be the encouragement.
