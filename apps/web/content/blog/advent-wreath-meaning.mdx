In the dead of winter, the ancient Germans lit wheels of fire to ask the sun god to return.
Christians baptized this tradition.
The Wreath is a circle (Eternity/God has no beginning or end) made of evergreens (Life amidst death).

## The 4 Candles
They represent the 4,000 years of waiting for the Messiah.
1. **Week 1 (Purple): Hope.** The Patriarchs.
2. **Week 2 (Purple): Peace.** The Prophets.
3. **Week 3 (Pink/Rose): Joy.** (Gaudete Sunday). "Rejoice!" The shepherds.
4. **Week 4 (Purple): Love.** The Angels.

## Why Pink?
Because in the middle of a penitential season (purple is the color of fasting), the Church says: *"Relax! He is almost here."*
It is the liturgical color of dawn.
