On Epiphany, Catholics chalk the lintel of their front door.
The formula changes every year.
For 2026, it is:
**20 + C + M + B + 26**

## The Meaning
- **numbers**: The current year (2026).
- **C M B**:
  1. The Three Kings: **C**asper, **M**elchior, **B**althasar.
  2. The Latin Prayer: ***C**hristus **M**ansionem **B**enedicat* ("May Christ Bless this House").
- **+ (Crosses)**: Represents the 4 seasons / 4 directions.

## Steps
1. Get blessed chalk from your parish.
2. Gather the family at the front door.
3. Mark the lintel while praying: *"Lord God of heaven and earth, reveal your Son to every guest who enters this door..."*
