On Feb 14, while you are eating chocolate, the Liturgical Calendar celebrates **Sts. Cyril and Methodius**.
They were brothers from Greece who went to mission to the Slavic peoples (Moravia/Russia).

## The Alphabet
The Slavs had no written language. So Cyril invented one: **Cyrillic** (still used in Russia/Ukraine/Bulgaria today).
He did it so he could translate the Bible and Liturgy into their native tongue.
They proved that the Gospel can speak any language.
