First rule of Exorcism: **Don't look for the devil under every rock.**
Possession is extremely rare.
Most spiritual problems are **Obsession** (mental torment) or **Oppression** (external attacks), not full Possession.

## What is an Exorcism?
It is a public prayer of the Church, using the authority of Jesus Christ, to expel demons from a person or object.
- **Minor Exorcism**: Used at Baptism (reverently).
- **Major Exorcism**: Used only for possessed persons by a priest mandated by the Bishop.

## The Signs
According to the Roman Ritual, signs of possession include:
1. Speaking in languages unknown to the person (e.g., Latin/Aramaic).
2. Superhuman strength.
3. Knowledge of hidden things (reading the priest's sins).
4. Aversion to holy objects (recoiling from Holy Water).

## The Process
Before an exorcism is approved, the person must see a **psychiatrist** and a **medical doctor**. We must rule out Schizophrenia, Tourette's, or Epilepsy first. Faith and Science work together.

The exorcist doesn't "fight" the demon with his own power. He commands it to leave *"In the name of Jesus Christ."* It is boring, repetitive prayer, not a movie scene.
