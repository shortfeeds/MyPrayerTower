St. Ignatius of Loyola taught us the **Examen**.
Normally, we do it at night. But you can do it for the whole year.

## The 5 Steps (Annual Edition)
1.  **Light**: Ask the Holy Spirit to show you the year as He sees it.
2.  **Thanksgiving**: Scroll through your calendar month by month. What were the highlights? (The vacation, the promotion, the baby). Thank Him.
3.  **Feelings**: Look for the lowlights. Where were you sad, angry, or distant from God? (The breakup, the failure). Don't judge it. Just feel it with Him.
4.  **Sorrow**: Where did you say "No" to God? Ask forgiveness.
5.  **Hope**: Look at the year ahead. What is one thing you are looking forward to?
