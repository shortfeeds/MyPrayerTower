On **October 7, 1571**, the future of Western Civilization hung in the balance.
The Ottoman Empire possessed the most powerful navy in the world. They were sailing West to conquer Rome.
The Christian Holy League was outnumbered, divided, and terrified.

## The Weapon
Pope St. Pius V didn't just send ships. He sent **Rosaries**.
He ordered all of Europe to pray the Rosary for victory.
On the flagships, the soldiers prayed. Back home, the monks and nuns prayed.

## The Wind Change
At the critical moment of the battle, the wind suddenly shifted 180 degrees. The smoke from the cannons blew back into the faces of the Ottoman fleet.
The Christians won a decisive victory.

## The Vision
At the exact moment of victory, thousands of miles away in Rome, the Pope stood up from a meeting, looked out the window, and said: *"Let us stop business. Our great fleet has won. We must thank God."*
He established the Feast of **Our Lady of Victory**, which was later renamed **Our Lady of the Rosary**.
