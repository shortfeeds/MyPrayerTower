# Who Is My Patron Saint?

A patron saint is a holy person in heaven who advocates for a specific cause, profession, or individual. They are chosen as special intercessors because they have walked the path of holiness before us.

## Why Do We Need Them?
We don't *need* them like we need God, but God gives them to us as family. Just as you ask a friend to pray for you, we ask the saints to pray for us. Because they are in heaven, their prayers are perfect and untainted by selfishness.

## Finding Your Patron
Many people choose a patron saint for:
1.  **Confirmation:** A name taken at the sacrament of Confirmation.
2.  **Profession:** Accountants (St. Matthew), Doctors (St. Luke), Musicians (St. Cecilia).
3.  **Struggle:** Anxiety (St. Dymphna), Lost Causes (St. Jude), Cancer (St. Peregrine).
4.  **Inspiration:** Someone whose life story resonates with yours.

## Signs a Saint is Choosing You
Often, it feels like the saint chooses *you*.
-   You keep hearing their name.
-   You receive a prayer card or medal of them unexpectedly.
-   Their feast day lands on a significant date for you.
-   You read their story and feel a deep connection.

## What to Do Once You Find Them
-   **Read their biography:** Know their story.
-   **Ask for their intercession:** "St. [Name], pray for me."
-   **Imitate their virtues:** If they were patient, practice patience.
-   **Celebrate their feast day:** Go to **Mass** or do something special.

The saints want to help us get to heaven. They are our cheerleaders in the great cloud of witnesses.
