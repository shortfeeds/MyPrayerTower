# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 25, 2024**

MyPrayerTower ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, mobile applications, and services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Name, email, password, phone number
- **Profile Information:** Profile photo, denomination preference, home church
- **Prayer Content:** Prayer requests, intentions, and testimonies you submit
- **Payment Information:** Billing address, payment method (processed by Stripe)
- **Communications:** Messages you send to us or other users

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, time spent, app crashes
- **Location Data:** With your permission, approximate or precise location
- **Cookies:** Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Social Login:** If you sign in with Google/Apple, we receive basic profile info
- **Church Data:** Publicly available church information from directories

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve our Services
- Process payments and subscriptions
- Moderate content and maintain safety
- Send notifications (with your consent)
- Personalize your experience
- Respond to your inquiries
- Analyze usage and trends
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 Public Content
Prayer requests marked "public" are visible to all users. Anonymous submissions show content without your name.

### 3.2 Service Providers
We share data with trusted providers for:
- Payment processing (Stripe)
- Email delivery (SendGrid)
- SMS notifications (Twilio)
- Analytics (Google Analytics)
- Hosting (AWS/Cloudflare)

### 3.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

### 3.4 Business Transfers
In case of merger, acquisition, or sale, user data may be transferred to the new owner.

## 4. Your Rights and Choices

### 4.1 Access and Portability
You can access and download your data through account settings.

### 4.2 Correction
Update your profile information at any time in account settings.

### 4.3 Deletion
Request account deletion by contacting privacy@myprayertower.com. We will delete your data within 30 days, except where retention is legally required.

### 4.4 Opt-Out
- **Marketing Emails:** Unsubscribe link in each email
- **Push Notifications:** Disable in app or device settings
- **Cookies:** Manage in browser settings

### 4.5 Location Data
You can disable location access in device settings.

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption:** TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access, regular audits
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and logging
- **Compliance:** Regular security assessments

While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide Services. After account deletion:
- Personal data: Deleted within 30 days
- Anonymized analytics: May be retained indefinitely
- Legal records: Retained as required by law

## 7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our Services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

For users aged 13-17, parental consent may be required for certain features.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using our Services, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards for international transfers.

## 9. Your Rights (Specific Regions)

### 9.1 European Union (GDPR)
EU residents have rights to:
- Access their personal data
- Rectify inaccurate data
- Erase data ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict or object to processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent
- Lodge complaints with supervisory authorities

### 9.2 California (CCPA)
California residents have rights to:
- Know what data is collected
- Request deletion
- Opt-out of sale (we do not sell personal data)
- Non-discrimination for exercising rights

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@myprayertower.com.

### 10.2 Cookie Management
