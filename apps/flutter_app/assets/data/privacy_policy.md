## 1. Introduction

Welcome to My Prayer Tower ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
- Name (when provided for prayer requests)
- Email address (for account creation)
- Prayer intentions and requests you submit

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- Device type and operating system
- App usage statistics
- Push notification preferences
- Anonymous analytics data

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- Location data
- Contact lists
- Photos or media files
- Financial information (payments are handled by Google Play)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain our app services
- Display prayer requests on the Prayer Wall (with your consent)
- Send push notification reminders for prayers
- Improve user experience
- Respond to inquiries and support requests

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- We use Supabase as our backend provider with enterprise-grade security
- Prayer requests marked as anonymous will not display your name
- We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information

## 5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:
- **Firebase:** For push notifications
- **Google AdMob:** For displaying advertisements
- **Google Play Billing:** For in-app purchases
- **Supabase:** For secure data storage

Each third-party service has its own Privacy Policy governing the use of your information.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for users of all ages as it provides Catholic prayer content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt-out of push notifications
- Withdraw consent for data processing

## 8. Push Notifications

We send push notifications to remind you about:
- Morning and evening prayers
- Daily readings and reflections
- Special feast days and celebrations

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
