**St. Thomas Aquinas** (1225-1274). His classmates called him the "Dumb Ox" because he was big and quiet.
His teacher, St. Albert the Great, said: *"This ox will bellow so loud he will be heard by the world."*

## The 5 Ways (A Summary)
You don't need faith to know God exists. You just need logic.
1. **Motion**: Someone had to push the first domino (The Unmoved Mover).
2. **Causality**: Effect needs a Cause. The First Cause is God.
3. **Contingency**: Things exist that didn't *have* to exist (like you). There must be a Necessary Being that holds them in existence.
4. **Gradation**: We know "Good, Better, Best." There must be a standard of Perfection.
5. **Design**: The universe has order (gravity, DNA). Order requires an Intelligent Designer.
