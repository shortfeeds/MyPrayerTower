Millions of Christians face persecution worldwide. Here's how to help.

---

## The Reality

### Persecution Is Real

From the Middle East to Africa to Asia, Christians face violence, imprisonment, and discrimination for their faith.

### The Church Suffer

"If they persecuted Me, they will persecute you" (John 15:20).

---

## How to Help

### 1. Pray

Prayer is powerful. Pray for the persecuted.

### 2. Advocate

Speak up. Contact representatives. Support organizations.

### 3. Give

Support organizations helping persecuted Christians.
