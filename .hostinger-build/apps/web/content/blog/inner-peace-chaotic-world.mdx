The world feels chaotic—politics, news, division, uncertainty. How do we find peace?

---

## The Source of Peace

### Not From the World

The world's peace is fragile—based on circumstances, safety, prosperity.

### From God

God's peace is different—steadfast, unshakable, eternal.

> "Peace I leave with you; my peace I give to you" (John 14:27).

---

## How to Find It

### 1. Prayer

Turn to God in prayer. He is our refuge.

### 2. Focus on What You Can Control

You can't control the world—but you can control your response.

### 3. Limit News

Too much news feeds anxiety. Limit intake.

### 4. Serve Others

Getting outside yourself brings peace.

---

## A Prayer for Peace

> *Lord, in the chaos, give me peace. Help me to trust You. Give me the grace to be calm. Amen.*

---

## Conclusion: Peace Is Possible

Even in chaos, peace is possible—with God.

*Lord, give me Your peace. Amen.*
