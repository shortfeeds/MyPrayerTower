**St. Vincent de Paul** (1581-1660) had a crazy life.
As a young priest, he was captured by Barbary pirates and sold into slavery in Tunisia. He converted his owner to Christianity and escaped back to France.

## The Shift
He used to hang out with royalty. But after hearing the confession of a dying peasant, he realized the spiritual poverty of the countryside.
He founded the **Congregation of the Mission** (Vincentians) and the **Daughters of Charity** (with St. Louise de Marillac).

## His Philosophy
> *"Go to the poor: you will find God."*
He taught that we must serve the poor with "sweetness and respect," because **they are our masters**. We are the servants.
Today, the St. Vincent de Paul Society is one of the largest charitable organizations in the world.
