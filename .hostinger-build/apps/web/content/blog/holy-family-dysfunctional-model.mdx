We see them on Christmas cards: Calm, clean, glowing.
The reality was messy:
1. **Scandal**: Mary was pregnant before marriage. Joseph almost divorced her.
2. **Homelessness**: No room at the inn. Gave birth in a barn.
3. **Refugees**: Had to flee to Egypt to escape a death squad.
4. **Panic**: Lost 12-year-old Jesus in Jerusalem for 3 days.

## Why It Matters
If your family is stressed, broke, or under attack... you fit right in.
The Holy Family isn't a model of "ease." They are a model of **trust** in the middle of chaos.
