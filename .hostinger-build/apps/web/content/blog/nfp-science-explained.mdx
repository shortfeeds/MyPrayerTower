Most people think NFP is guessing.
**Fact:** Modern methods (Creighton, Marquette, Sympto-Thermal) monitor biological markers (hormones, temperature, mucus) to pinpoint ovulation within hours.
It is **fertility awareness**, not guesswork.

## Why Not Contraception?
Contraception says: *"I give myself to you... except for my fertility."* (Conditional love).
NFP says: *"I give myself to you completely. But we will abstain this week for a serious reason."* (Sacrificial love).
One breaks the procreative power; the other respects it.
