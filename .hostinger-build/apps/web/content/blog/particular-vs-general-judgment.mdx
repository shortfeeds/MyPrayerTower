**Myth**: When you die, you sleep until the end of the world.
**Fact**: When you die, your soul is judged *instantly*.

## 1. Particular Judgment
The moment your soul leaves your body, you stand before Christ.
There is no jury. There are no witnesses. Just you and the Truth.
Your life will flash before your eyes—not as a movie, but as God sees it. Every hidden act, every secret thought.
**Result**: Heaven (possibly via Purgatory) or Hell. This is permanent.

## 2. General Judgment (The Last Day)
At the end of time, all bodies will appear before Christ (Resurrection of the Body).
Why judge us again?
To reveal the **ripple effects** of our lives.
- Your good deed might have inspired someone 500 years later.
- Your bad example might have led generations into sin.
The General Judgment reveals God's Justice to the whole universe.
