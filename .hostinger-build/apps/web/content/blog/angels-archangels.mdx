Angels are real—and important in Catholic teaching. Here's what the Church teaches.

---

## What Are Angels?

### Created Beings

Angels are pure spirits—created beings (not "gods").

### Their Nature

They have intelligence and will—but no bodies (though they can appear in bodily form).

---

## The Archangels

### The Three Named

- **Michael:** "Who is like God?"—the warrior who defeated Satan
- **Gabriel:** "God is my strength"—the messenger (to Daniel, Mary)
- ** Raphael:** "God has healed"—the healer (in Tobit)

---

## The Angelic Hierarchy

### Nine Choirs

The Church teaches there are nine choirs (ranks) of angels:

1. Seraphim
2. Cherubim
3. Thrones
4. Dominations
5. Principalities
6. Powers
7. Virtues
8. Archangels
9. Angels

---

## Guardian Angels

### Each Person Has One

Jesus says: "See that you do not despise one of these little ones. For I tell you that their angels in heaven always see the face of my Father in heaven" (Matthew 10:10).

---

## Conclusion: Real Beings

Angels are real—spiritual beings who serve God and assist us.

*Lord, thank You for my guardian angel. Amen.*
