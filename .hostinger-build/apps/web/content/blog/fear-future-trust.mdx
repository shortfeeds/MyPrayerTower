Fear of the future can be paralyzing. But we don't have to live in fear.

---

## The Truth About the Future

### God Is in Control

God knows the future. He's already there. He's already in charge.

### He Loves Us

Whatever happens, He loves us. His love is steadfast.

---

## How to Trust

### 1. Remember the Past

God has gotten you through every day so far. He'll continue.

### 2. Focus on Today

Don't borrow tomorrow's problems. Live today.

### 3. Offer It Up

Whatever the future holds, offer it to God.

---

## A Prayer

> *Lord, I don't know what the future holds. But I trust You. Take care of me. Amen.*

---

## Conclusion: Trust Wins

Trust defeats fear. Choose trust.

*Lord, increase my trust in You. Amen.*
