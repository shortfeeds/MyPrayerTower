Raising Catholic kids in today's world is challenging. But it's possible. Here's how.

---

## The Challenge

### The Secular World

Our world is increasingly secular—anti-Christian in subtle and not-so-subtle ways.

### The Pressure

Kids face pressure at school, online, and in peer groups. They're influenced by the culture.

---

## Practical Solutions

### 1. Prayer

Teach your kids to pray—daily, personally. Make prayer natural.

### 2. Mass

Make Mass attendance non-negotiable. It's the source of the faith.

### 3. Confession

Regular Confession cleanses the soul. Help kids go.

### 4. Role Model

Live your faith. Kids watch what you do more than what you say.

### 5. Communication

Talk about faith—casually, naturally, often.

### 6. Community

Surround your kids with Catholic friends. It matters.

---

## Dealing with Challenges

### When They Question

Encourage questions. Don't shut them down.

### When They Rebel

Pray. Don't give up. Persevere.

### When They Leave

Keep loving. Keep praying. Keep the door open.

---

## A Prayer for Parents

> *Lord, give me the grace to raise my children in the faith. Help me to be patient, wise, and faithful. Amen.*

---

## Conclusion: It's a Long Game

Parenting is a long game. Keep praying. Keep going.

*Lord, bless our parenting. Amen.*
