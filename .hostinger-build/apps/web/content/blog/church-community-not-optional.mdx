Being "spiritual but not religious" is popular. But Catholics know: we need community. Here's why.

---

## The Problem with "Spiritual But Not Religious"

### It's Lonely

Private faith isolates. It's hard to stay strong alone.

### It's Dangerous

Without community, we can drift into error.

### It's Incomplete

We're made for relationship—with God and others.

---

## Why Community Matters

### 1. The Church Is the Body

We're part of the Body of Christ—not isolated individuals.

### 2. We Need Each Other

We need others to teach, correct, encourage, support.

### 3. Grace Flows Through the Church

The Sacraments, the community, the Church—these are channels of grace.

---

## The Solution

### 1. Go to Mass

Be present at the source.

### 2. Join a Group

Find a small group, ministry, or community.

### 3. Serve

Use your gifts in the Church.

---

## Conclusion: You're Part of Something

You're not alone. You're part of the Body.

*Lord, help me to be part of the community. Amen.*
