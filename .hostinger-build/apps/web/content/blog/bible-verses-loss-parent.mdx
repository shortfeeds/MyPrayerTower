Losing a parent is one of the most profound losses we can experience. The Bible offers comfort for those navigating this difficult journey.

Here are powerful verses to hold onto.

---

## For the Grief Itself

### Psalm 34:18

> "The Lord is near to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit."

God is close to us in our pain.

### Psalm 30:5

> "Weeping may linger for the night, but joy comes with the morning."

Grief has a limit. Joy will come.

### John 14:1

> "Do not let your hearts be troubled. Believe in God; believe also in me."

Jesus knows our pain—and offers comfort.

---

## For Hope

### 1 Thessalonians 4:13-14

> "We do not want you to be ignorant, brothers and sisters, about those who fall asleep, so that you may not grieve like the rest of mankind, who have no hope. For we believe that Jesus died and rose again, so we believe that God will bring with Jesus those who have fallen asleep in him."

We grieve, but not without hope.

### Romans 8:28

> "We know that all things work together for good for those who love God, who are called according to his purpose."

Even loss works for good.

### 2 Corinthians 4:17

> "For this momentary light affliction is producing for us an eternal weight of glory far beyond all comparison."

Our suffering has meaning.

---

## For the Love of God

### Psalm 23:4

> "Even though I walk through the darkest valley, I fear no evil; for you are with me; your rod and your staff—they comfort me."

God walks with us through the valley.

### Isaiah 41:10

> "Do not fear, for I am with you; do not be afraid, for I am your God. I will strengthen you, help you, and uphold you with my righteous right hand."

We are never alone.

### Lamentations 3:31-32

> "For the Lord will not reject forever. For he causes grief, but then has compassion, according to his abundant loving-kindness."

God's love is steadfast.

---

## For Eternal Life

### John 11:25-26

> "Jesus said to her, 'I am the resurrection and the life; whoever believes in me, even if he dies, will live. Everyone who lives and believes in me will never die.'"

Death is not the end.

### 1 Peter 1:3-4

> "Blessed be the God and Father of our Lord Jesus Christ, who according to his great mercy has given us a new birth into a living hope through the resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead, into an inheritance that is imperishable, undefiled, and unfading, kept in heaven for you."

We have an eternal inheritance.

---

## For Strength

### Isaiah 40:29

> "He gives power to the faint, and to him who has no might he increases strength."

God gives us strength.

### Philippians 4:13

> "I can do all things through him who gives me strength."

With God, we can endure.

---

## Conclusion: Hold Onto These Words

When grief feels overwhelming, hold onto these verses. Let them sink into your heart. Let them remind you: God is with you. Hope is real. This is not the end.

*Lord, thank You for Your Word. Thank You for Your comfort. Help me to hold onto these promises. Amen.*
