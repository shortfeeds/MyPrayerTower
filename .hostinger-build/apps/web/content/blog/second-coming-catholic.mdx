The end of the world is coming—but it's not something to fear. Here's what Catholics believe about the Second Coming.

---

## What Is the Second Coming?

### The Return of Christ

Jesus, who ascended into heaven, will return—at the end of time, in glory.

### When?

No one knows the exact time—but it will happen.

> "But of that day or hour, no one knows, neither the angels in heaven, nor the Son, but only the Father" (Mark 13:32).

---

## What Will Happen?

### 1. The Resurrections

All the dead will rise—bodies raised, united with souls.

### 2. The Judgment

The Final Judgment—each person judged by Christ.

### 3. The New Creation

A new heavens and new earth—"God will dwell with them" (Revelation 21:3).

---

## The Catholic Hope

### 1. Victory

The Second Coming isn't defeat—it's victory. Christ's return marks the triumph of good over evil.

### 2. Restoration

Everything broken will be fixed. Every tear will be wiped away (Revelation 21:4).

### 3. Eternity

We will live forever with God—not in a "cloud," but in a restored creation.

---

## How to Prepare

### 1. Be Faithful

Live your faith daily. Be ready.

### 2. Stay Vigilant

Don't be caught off guard.

### 3. Hope

Live with hope—not fear.

---

## Conclusion: Maranatha

"Come, Lord Jesus" (Revelation 22:20). That's our prayer.

*Lord, come again in glory. Make all things new. Amen.*
