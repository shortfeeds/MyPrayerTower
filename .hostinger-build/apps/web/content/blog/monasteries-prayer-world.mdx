Monks and nuns in cloistered monasteries may seem "out of the world"—but they're supporting it in a powerful way.

---

## The Contemplative Life

### What It Is

Contemplative monks and nuns live entirely for God—dedicated to prayer, work, and enclosure.

### The Vocation

It's a hidden vocation—but vital to the Church.

---

## How They Help

### 1. Constant Prayer

They're praying constantly—for the Church, for the world, for you.

### 2. Sacrifice

Their lives are sacrifices—offered for others.

### 3. Intercession

They're intercessors—bringing our needs to God.

---

## How to Support Them

### 1. Pray for Them

They're supported by our prayers.

### 2. Visit

Some monasteries allow visits.

### 3. Donate

Support their work.

---

## Conclusion: Hidden But Powerful

The contemplative life is hidden—but powerful.

*Lord, bless all monks and nuns. May their prayers support us. Amen.*
