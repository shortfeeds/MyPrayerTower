Archbishop **Fulton Sheen** wrote a masterpiece called *Three to Get Married*.
His thesis: **1 + 1 = 2 is wrong.**
In a Catholic marriage, 1 + 1 + 1 = 1.
You + Spouse + God.

## The Glue
Human love runs out. We get tired, wrinkly, and annoying.
Divine Love (the 3rd Person) is infinite. When your tank is empty, you draw from His.
*"It takes three to make love, not two: you, you, and God. Without God people only succeed in bringing out the worst in one another."*
