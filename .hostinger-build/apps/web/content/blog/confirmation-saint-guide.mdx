One of the most important parts of Catholic Confirmation is choosing your Confirmation saint—a patron who will accompany you on your spiritual journey.

But how do you choose? What should you consider? Let's explore.

---

## Why Have a Confirmation Saint?

The tradition comes from the early Church: sponsors (those who present the confirmand) would choose a saint to be the "patron"—someone to pray for the person throughout their life.

The saint becomes:
- **A spiritual friend** to pray to
- **A model** to imitate
- **An intercessor** in heaven

---

## How to Choose

### 1. Consider Your Life Experiences

- What struggles have you faced?
- What are you passionate about?
- What do you want to grow in?

### 2. Look at Your Name

Your "name saint" is a natural choice—if your name is "Michael," St. Michael is an option. But you can choose any saint.

### 3. Consider Your Interests

- Do you love sports? St. Sebastian might be for you
- Do you care about the poor? St. Vincent de Paul
- Are you shy? St. Joseph
- Do you love learning? St. Thomas Aquinas

### 4. Feel Drawn

Sometimes, you just "feel" drawn to a saint. Trust that feeling.

### 5. Research

Learn about different saints. Read their stories. See who resonates.

---

## Popular Confirmation Saints

### For Strength: St. Michael the Archangel
Patron of soldiers, police, and paramedics.

### For Wisdom: St. Thomas Aquinas
Patron of students, philosophers, and academics.

### For Courage: St. Joan of Arc
Patron of soldiers, those discriminated against.

### For Purity: St. Maria Goretti
Patron of youth, purity, and young women.

### For Help in Desperation: St. Jude Thaddeus
Patron of impossible causes.

### For Family: St. Joseph
Patron of fathers, workers, and the dying.

### For the Eucharist: St. Carlo Acutis
Patron of youth, the Eucharist.

---

## The Process

### 1. Research

Spend time learning about saints. Use books, websites, or ask your priest.

### 2. Pray

Ask God to guide you. Ask saints to reveal themselves to you.

### 3. Choose

Pick one—or let the saint pick you!

### 4. Prepare

Learn about your saint's life. Prepare to explain why you chose them.

---

## What to Do After Confirmation

### 1. Learn More

Continue learning about your saint.

### 2. Pray to Them

Add your saint to your daily prayer life.

### 3. Imitate Them

Try to live the virtues they embodied.
