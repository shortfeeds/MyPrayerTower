A lot of Catholics get their theology from *Left Behind* movies.
Here is the problem: **The Rapture is not Catholic.**
(The idea that Jesus will secretly snatch believers away before the tribulation was invented in 1830 by John Nelson Darby).

## The Catholic Timeline (CCC 675-677)
1. **The Great Tribulation**: The Church will pass through a final trial that will shake the faith of many.
2. **The Antichrist**: A religious deception offering men an apparent solution to their problems at the price of apostasy. It will probably be a humanitarian, secular messianism.
3. **The Second Coming**: Christ returns **once**, visibly, in glory.
4. **The Resurrection of the Dead**: Everyone gets their bodies back.
5. **The Final Judgment**: Sheep vs. Goats.
6. **New Heavens & New Earth**.

We don't get "Left Behind." We get "Left Through." We suffer *with* Christ to reign with Him.
