Scripture says: *"At the name of Jesus every knee should bow, in heaven and on earth and under the earth."* (Phil 2:10).

## Why "Jesus"?
The name (Yeshua) means **"God Saves."**
It is not just a label. It contains the power of His presence.
When you whisper "Jesus" with love, you are praying the shortest, most powerful prayer in the world.

## The IHS Monogram
You see it on altars. It is not "I Have Suffered" or "In His Service."
It is simply the first three letters of Jesus' name in Greek: **I**ota-**E**ta-**S**igma.
**St. Bernardino of Siena** used to hold up a board with "IHS" surrounded by rays of light to calm fighting mobs in Italy.
