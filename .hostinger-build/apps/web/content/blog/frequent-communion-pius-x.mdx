In the early 20th century, Pope St. Pius X made a revolutionary recommendation: that Catholics receive Holy Communion daily.

Let's explore why.

---

## The Context

### 1905: "Sacra Tridentina"

In 1905, Pope St. Pius X issued the decree "Sacra Tridentina," encouraging frequent (even daily) Communion.

His reasoning: The Eucharist is the "source and summit" of the Christian life. Why would we only receive it weekly?

---

## The Benefits of Frequent Communion

### 1. Greater Union with Christ

Each Communion unites us more closely to Christ.

### 2. Spiritual Growth

Frequent Communion helps us grow in virtue.

### 3. Healing

The Eucharist heals our wounds—spiritual and sometimes physical.

### 4. Strength

It gives us the strength to live the Christian life.

---

## The Requirements

To receive frequently, you must:
- Be in a state of grace (no mortal sin)
- Have the proper disposition
- Not be receiving unworthily
