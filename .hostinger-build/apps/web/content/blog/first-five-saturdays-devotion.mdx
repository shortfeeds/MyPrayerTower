In 1917 at Fatima, Our Lady told the shepherd children: *"God wishes to establish in the world devotion to my Immaculate Heart."*

Later, in 1925, she appeared to Lucia and revealed exactly how: **The Communion of Reparation on the First Saturdays.** She promised to assist at the hour of death with all graces necessary for salvation those who practice this devotion.

## The 4 Conditions
You must do the following **on the First Saturday of 5 consecutive months**:

1. **Go to Confession** (Within 8 days before or after).
2. **Receive Holy Communion** (In a state of grace).
3. **Pray 5 Decades of the Rosary**.
4. **Keep Mary Company for 15 Minutes** while meditating on the mysteries of the Rosary.

**Crucial**: All of these must be offered **with the intention of making reparation** to the Immaculate Heart of Mary.

## Why 5 Saturdays?
Jesus revealed to Lucia that there are 5 kinds of offenses against Mary's Immaculate Heart:
1. Blasphemies against her **Immaculate Conception**.
2. Blasphemies against her **Perpetual Virginity**.
3. Blasphemies against her **Divine Maternity** (refusing to recognize her as Mother of God and Mother of men).
4. Offenses by those who **instill indifference or hatred** for her in children's hearts.
5. Offenses by those who **outrage her directly in her holy images**.

## The Meditation (15 Minutes)
This is separate from the Rosary itself. It is "keeping Mary company." You can:
- Read Scripture related to one mystery (e.g., the Nativity) for 15 minutes.
- Meditate silently on the 15 mysteries.
- Talk to Mary about her life and sorrows.

## FAQ

### What if I miss a month?
You (usually) have to start the count of 5 over again. The point is perseverance and consistency.

### Can I go to Confession on Saturday evening?
Yes. Confession can be up to 8 days before or after, as long as you are in a state of grace when you receive Communion on Saturday.

### Why does reparation matter?
Can we really "repair" damage done to Mary? Yes. Just as a child can comfort a mother who has been insulted by another child, our love "consoles" Mary's heart and opens floodgates of grace for the conversion of sinners (especially in Russia, as prophesied).
