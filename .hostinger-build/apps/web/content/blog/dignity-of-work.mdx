Your work is more than a paycheck—it's a calling. Here's how to find God in it.

---

## The Dignity of Work

### Work Is Holy

Work isn't a curse—it's a gift. Even after the Fall, work remains valuable.

### Work As Worship

Whatever you do, do it for God's glory (Colossians 3:23).

---

## Finding God at Work

### 1. Do It Well

Work excellently. God is honored when we do our best.

### 2. Offer It Up

Offer your work as a sacrifice to God—even the mundane parts.

### 3. Serve Others

View your coworkers and customers as people to serve, not just obligations.

---

## A Prayer for Work

> *Lord, help me to find You in my work. May everything I do bring glory to Your name. Amen.*

---

## Conclusion: It's All Sacred

No work is "just" work. It's all sacred when offered to God.

*Lord, make my work holy. Amen.*
