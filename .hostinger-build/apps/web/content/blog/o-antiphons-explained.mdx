If you sing "O Come, O Come Emmanuel," you know the **O Antiphons**.
They are short prayers recited before the Magnificat during Vespers in the final week of Advent.

## The 7 Titles
1. **O Sapientia** (Wisdom) - Dec 17
2. **O Adonai** (Lord) - Dec 18
3. **O Radix Jesse** (Root of Jesse) - Dec 19
4. **O Clavis David** (Key of David) - Dec 20
5. **O Oriens** (Rising Sun) - Dec 21
6. **O Rex Gentium** (King of Nations) - Dec 22
7. **O Emmanuel** (God with Us) - Dec 23

## The Code
Take the first letter of each Latin title in reverse:
**E**mmanuel, **R**ex, **O**riens, **C**lavis, **R**adix, **A**donai, **S**apientia.
**ERO CRAS**.
Translation: *"Tomorrow, I will be there."*
The monks who arranged this were geniuses.
