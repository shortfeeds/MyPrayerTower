**St. Francis de Sales** (1567-1622) was the Bishop of Geneva (Calvinist territory).
People wouldn't listen to his sermons. So he started slipping tracts under their doors at night. (The first thumb-scroll marketing).

## His Motto
> *"You catch more flies with a spoonful of honey than with a barrel of vinegar."*
He was incredibly gentle. He taught that holiness is not just for monks. It is for soldiers, mothers, and shopkeepers.
His book *Introduction to the Devout Life* was the first self-help book for lay Catholics.

## Why Journalists?
Because he used the media of his day (printed tracts) to spread the truth.
