One of the most powerful things a married couple can do is pray together. Here's how to start.

---

## Why Pray Together?

### Unity

Prayer brings couples together—spiritually, emotionally.

### Protection

"Praying together, staying together." Couples who pray together are less likely to divorce.

---

## How to Start

### 1. Keep It Simple

Don't overcomplicate. Start with a blessing before meals.

### 2. Add a Decade

After meals, add one decade of the Rosary.

### 3. Build Up

As it becomes natural, add more.

---

## A Couple's Prayer

> *Lord, strengthen our marriage through prayer. Help us to pray together, always. Amen.*

---

## Conclusion: Start Today

Don't wait. Start praying together today.

*Lord, bless our marriage with prayer. Amen.*
