"Let go and let God" is popular—but what does it actually mean? Let's explore.

---

## What It Means

### Surrender to God's Will

It means accepting God's will—not as a backup plan, but as the plan.

### Stop Trying to Control

It means we stop trying to control everything—our outcomes, other people, the future.

---

## Why It's Hard

### We Like Control

We think we know what's best. We want to be in control.

### Fear

We fear what happens if we let go.

---

## How to Do It

### 1. Accept

Accept that God's will is best—even when we don't understand it.

### 2. Release

Let go of the things we can't control. We can't control people, outcomes, the future.

### 3. Trust

Trust that God is good—and that He's in control.

---

## A Prayer of Surrender

> *Lord, I let go. I release my need to control. I trust You. Be in charge of my life. Amen.*

---

## Conclusion: Freedom in Surrender

Letting go isn't giving up—it's finding freedom.

*Lord, help me to let go and let You. Amen.*
