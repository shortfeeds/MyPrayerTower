# Community Guidelines

**Last Updated: December 25, 2024**

Welcome to the MyPrayerTower community! These guidelines ensure a safe, respectful, and uplifting environment for all members.

## Our Core Values

MyPrayerTower is built on:
- **Faith** - Supporting spiritual growth
- **Respect** - Treating all with dignity
- **Compassion** - Showing kindness to those in need
- **Community** - Building connections across borders

## Prayer Wall Guidelines

### Acceptable Content
✅ Personal prayer intentions and requests
✅ Expressions of gratitude and thanksgiving
✅ Prayers for family, health, work, and spiritual growth
✅ Testimonies of answered prayers
✅ Requests for community support

### Prohibited Content
❌ Hate speech or discrimination
❌ Political statements or divisive content
❌ Explicit, obscene, or violent content
❌ Spam, advertising, or promotional material
❌ Personal attacks or harassment
❌ False or misleading information
❌ Content encouraging harmful behavior
❌ Sharing private information of others

## Respectful Interaction

When engaging with others:
- Use kind and supportive language
- Respect different prayer traditions within Christianity
- Avoid debates about doctrine or theology
- Report concerning content rather than engaging
- Remember there's a real person behind each prayer

## Privacy and Safety

- Never share personally identifiable information publicly
- Use "Anonymous" option for sensitive prayers
- Respect the privacy of others
- Report any suspicious or harmful behavior

## Church Listings

For churches:
- Provide accurate and current information
- Update Mass times and schedules regularly
- Respond professionally to inquiries
- Represent your church honestly

## Reporting Violations

If you see content that violates these guidelines:
1. Use the "Report" button on the content
2. Select the appropriate reason
3. Add any helpful context
4. Our moderation team will review within 24 hours

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Content removal
- Warning notification
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent account ban

Repeated or severe violations will result in immediate action.

## Appeals

If you believe your content was removed in error:
- Email: appeals@myprayertower.com
- Include: Your username, the content, and why you believe it was wrongly removed
- We will respond within 5 business days

## Stay Connected

Thank you for being part of our prayer community. Together, we can create a space of hope, healing, and spiritual growth.
